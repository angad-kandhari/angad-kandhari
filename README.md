## about me

Product Manager at **[Elenium Automation](https://www.elenium.com/)**, working on self-service and biometrics for airports: the kiosks, bag drops, and flows that make (or break) your trip before you even see a gate.

Outside of work I build local-first tools. The kind that live on my laptop, never phone home, and quietly make my day less annoying.

- ✈️ **Product @ Elenium** in Melbourne
- 🛠 **PM who ships code** on weekends
